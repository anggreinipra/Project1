About

At SunnyCo, we believe in making solar energy accessible, efficient, and hassle-free. Our cutting-edge solar systems are designed to seamlessly integrate into your home or business, providing you with reliable, clean energy that not only reduces your carbon footprint but also cuts down on your electricity bills. From the moment you reach out, our team is dedicated to guiding you through every step of the process—from personalized consultations and system design to professional installation and ongoing support. With SunnyCo, you’ll experience the benefits of solar energy in a way that’s simple, sustainable, and tailored to your needs. Whether you're looking to power your home, business, or community, our solar solutions provide a bright, affordable future powered by the sun.

Products

Ground Mounted Solar Power

This solar power plant is built on land using special supports to hold the solar panels. Ground-mounted solar power plants are typically connected to the utility grid to meet electricity needs in surrounding areas.

Industrial Rooftop Solar Power

This solar power plant utilizes the rooftops of factories or office buildings for installation. This system can be used to reduce electricity costs by generating power through solar panels.

Residential Rooftop Solar Power

This solar power plant uses the rooftops of residential buildings for installation. It can help reduce household electricity consumption by providing energy from solar panels. Additionally, rooftop solar power systems can enhance the aesthetic value of the property.

Solar Power for Telecommunication

This product is a solution for providing energy to telecommunication signal transmitters in areas lacking or not yet connected to a 24-hour electricity supply. The combination of solar panels and batteries can provide energy throughout the day.

Solar Street Lighting

Solar-powered street lighting and energy-efficient lamps are the perfect solution for utilizing renewable, pollution-free energy. The use of independent energy, without the need for grid electricity, to power the lamps is a key advantage of this product.
